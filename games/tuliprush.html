Tulip Rush

Year: 2025
Engine: Unity
Platform: Windows
My part: Programming, audio design

Tulip Rush is a game jam game made in two days for Finnish Game Jam 25 and Global Game Jam 25. The jam theme was "bubble".
The game takes place in 17th century Netherlands, where the world's first financial bubble called "Tulip Mania" happened.
You are a greedy merchant, and your goal is to make as much profit as possible, while manipulating the market by generating artificial scarcity and destroying competition.
On this project I mostly concentrated on programming, but I also sourced the sound effects.
