Doomsday Barista

Year: 2019
Engine: Adobe Animate
Platform: Android
My part: programming and game desing

Doomsday Barista is a game we made during the first year of our IT studies at Vuoksi in early 2019. It was created in about two months with Adobe Animate. I was responsible for all the programming, had a lot of practice for ActionScript3. We are planning on making this a full game someday Using unity.
The game has had a great legacy and the Gameschool banners etc. still have this game's graphics.

The game takes place in a post apocalyptic world. You are a hipster who found out about this ancient drink called coffee, and drives around the wasteland serving coffee to the people.
But in the wasteland an another beverage has a great foothold. Soon the boss of the Earl Grey Gang will find out what his subordinates have been drinking...
